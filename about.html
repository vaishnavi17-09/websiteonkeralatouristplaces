About Kerala

General Information

Kerala is a state located in the southwestern region of India along the Arabian Sea. It was formed on 1 November 1956. Kerala is known for high literacy rate, rich culture, festivals and scenic beauty.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
  • Language: Malayalam, English
  • Main Occupation: Agriculture, Fishing, Tourism
  • Major Festivals: Onam, Vishu, Thrissur Pooram
  • Famous Food: Appam, Puttu, Kerala Sadhya, Fish curry

Culture & Tradition

Kerala is known for classical art forms like Kathakali and Theyyam. Ayurveda treatment attracts global tourists. People are friendly and welcoming.

Climate

Kerala has tropical climate with heavy monsoon rains. Best time to visit is September to March.
